How to Request Deletion

If you would like us to delete your personal data associated with k, please send an email to apo@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request – k” and include:

  • Your name
  • The email address used in the app or with your login provider (e.g., Facebook)
  • Any additional information we may need to identify your account

What Will Be Deleted

Once we receive and verify your request, we will delete, or anonymize where necessary:

  • Account information
  • Personal profile details stored by us
  • Any other personal data associated with your account that we control

Retention of Some Data

In some cases, we may retain certain information where required by law, to resolve disputes, or to enforce our agreements. In such cases, we will retain only the minimum necessary information and keep it only for as long as required.

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at apo@gmail.com.
